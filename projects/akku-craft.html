Akku-Craft

Sustainable • Modular • Decentralized
Akku-Craft is a project designed to end the era of disposable black-box batteries. We are building a modular ecosystem that prioritizes repairability, scalability, and decentralized monitoring to reduce electronic waste.

GitHub Wiki

Project Overview

The current battery industry is often plagued by proprietary designs and planned obsolescence. Akku-Craft aims to break this cycle. We are building a future where battery systems are no longer disposable black boxes.

Instead of discarding an entire pack due to a single fault, our modular design allows for repair, scalability, and granular control. Our mission is to reduce electronic waste by creating a battery ecosystem where individual components are replaceable and intelligence is distributed.

Sustainable Modular Decentralized Arduino BMS Open Source

Sustainable

Designed for repairability and long lifecycles. We focus on maintenance to actively reduce electronic waste.

Modular

Scale your capacity by simply adding units, not by redesigning the system. The architecture grows with your needs.

Decentralized

Intelligence is distributed across the system. Decentralized monitoring ensures better safety, reliability, and granular control.

The Vision

Akku-Craft is a synergy of hardware, mechanics, and firmware, driven by a dedicated group of makers and engineers. We believe that openness and documentation are key to sustainable technology.

  • Reduce E-Waste: By designing for repairability and avoiding glued, proprietary assemblies.
  • Scalable Safety: Granular control allows for safer operation and easier identification of faulty cells.
  • Open Documentation: Schematics, 3D models, and code are open for anyone to learn from and improve.

How It Works

Hardware (Schematics)

The physical circuit logic and power distribution. Designed to be modular and robust to handle flexible energy needs.

Mechanics (3D Models)

The housing that keeps everything safe and portable. Custom modeled to fit the modular components perfectly.

Firmware (ArduinoBMS)

The "brain" of the system. Written in C++, it monitors voltage, current, and temperature to ensure safe operation.
