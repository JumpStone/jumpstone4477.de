JS Gaming

Enhance Your Game
JS Gaming is a project by JumpStone, dedicated to enhancing the Minecraft experience. We specialize in developing high-quality resource packs, optimized modpacks, and custom gameplay enhancements. Discover our collection of performance-driven tools and creative assets designed to take your game to the next level.

About

JS Gaming focuses on bringing quality and performance to the Minecraft community. Whether it is through carefully crafting resource packs or compiling balanced modpacks, our goal is to improve the player experience.

Development & Hosting

We believe in open development and transparency. Here is how we manage our projects:

  • Primary Platform: Our development happens mainly on GitHub. All pushes and active coding are done there.
  • Mirroring: All repositories are automatically mirrored to Codeberg to ensure availability and support open-source alternatives.
  • Issue Tracking: Please use our GitHub Issue Tracker for bug reports, suggestions, and feedback. We currently do not track issues on Codeberg.
